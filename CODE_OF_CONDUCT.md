# Code of Conduct

This is a maintainer-led personal project. Participation is welcome within the scope described below.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and constructive in issues and discussions.
- Describe problems clearly and provide enough context to be useful.
- Accept that proposals may be declined without detailed explanation.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Personal attacks, insults, or harassment directed at the maintainer or other participants.
- Spam, off-topic posts, or repeated requests after a decision has been made.

## Enforcement

The maintainer reserves the right to close issues or block accounts that violate these expectations, without prior warning.

## Scope

This code of conduct applies to all interactions in this repository, including issues, discussions, pull requests, and comments.
